My imperfections were fixed after nearly two years, I enrolled into a high school magnet, based on these programs. I was skeptical of the school, but quickly fell in love. Little did I realize this program would benefit my future major.
In the first eight months of being in this school, I was already certified in Adobe Illustrator. I became skilled at creating my own designs and posters. My class was taught how to illustrate, 3D design, and edit images. We learned how beneficial 3D printing is. For example, animals or people who have missing limbs can be granted the opportunity. After learning this, it kicked in how much I wanted to become a veterinarian. This high school program was opening the doors to different fields of study.
